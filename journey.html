Kebab Cultural Journey

A simple look at kebab traditions around the world.
Turkey
Döner Kebab
Rotating meat cooked on a vertical spit, served with pita and vegetables. A classic from the Ottoman Empire.
Greece
Souvlaki
Small pieces of meat grilled on skewers, often with pita and tzatziki. A favorite since ancient times.
India
Seekh Kebab
Minced meat with spices, grilled on skewers in a tandoor oven. A Mughal cuisine specialty.
Lebanon
Shish Kebab
Marinated lamb or beef grilled on skewers, served with tabbouleh and hummus.
Modern Fusion
Global Innovation
Chefs worldwide blend kebab traditions with local flavors for new creations.

Fun Kebab Facts

  • Kebabs have a history of over 2,000 years.
  • Almost every country has its own kebab style.
  • UNESCO recognizes Turkish döner as cultural heritage.
  • Kebabs unite people through food and tradition.
